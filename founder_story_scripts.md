# NoJob — Founder Story TikTok Scripts

---

## VERSION A: Face-to-Camera
**Format:** Talking directly to lens, casual/authentic tone
**Target length:** ~60–75 seconds
**Best for:** TikTok primary post, Instagram Reel

---

### SCRIPT

**[HOOK — first 2 seconds, before they scroll]**
> "I applied to over a hundred jobs. I have no idea how many I actually heard back from."

*[pause, slight laugh]*

**[THE PROBLEM]**
> "I had applications in spreadsheets, in my notes app, in my email, in my head.
> I was applying to the same companies twice. Missing follow-up deadlines.
> Completely losing track of where I even was in the process."

*[slight shift in tone — more direct]*

**[THE PIVOT]**
> "And the worst part wasn't the rejections. It was the chaos. I felt like I was doing
> a ton of work and had nothing to show for it. No progress. No momentum."

**[THE BUILD]**
> "So I built something."

*[brief pause — let it land]*

> "I made an app called NoJob. It tracks every application you send,
> but it works like a game. You earn points. You unlock achievements.
> You can add your friends and compete on a leaderboard."

**[THE SHOW — hold up phone or cut to screen record]**
> "Because the job hunt already feels like a grind — it might as well
> feel like one you're winning."

**[CTA — casual, not salesy]**
> "It's free. Link in bio. And if you're in the middle of a job search right now —
> I made this for you."

---

**On-screen text overlays (add in editing):**
- Hook: "100+ applications. Zero system." (appears at 0:00)
- At pivot: "so I built something" (appears as you say it)
- App name reveal: "NoJob" with gradient text
- End card: "Free at nojobapp.com"

**Suggested sounds:** Start with no sound or ambient, switch to a trending lo-fi/motivational sound when showing the app. Or use the whole video with no music — raw authenticity often outperforms.

## VERSION B: Voiceover + Screen Recording
**Format:** Your voice narrating over footage of the app in action
**Target length:** ~45–60 seconds
**Best for:** Lower barrier to film, great for showing off features

---

### SCRIPT

**[HOOK — over a blank screen or chaos visual like a messy notes app]**
> "I applied to over a hundred jobs and had absolutely no idea what I was doing."

**[PROBLEM — show a messy spreadsheet or just black screen]**
> "I was tracking applications in a spreadsheet. Then I wasn't.
> Missing follow-ups. Applying to the same jobs twice.
> I had no system and it was costing me real opportunities."

**[TRANSITION — cut to NoJob app opening]**
> "So I built NoJob."

**[SHOW THE APP — screen record each feature as you name it]**
> "It's a job tracker — but it works like a game."

*[show dashboard]*
> "Every application you send earns you points."

*[show achievements screen]*
> "You unlock achievements as you go."

*[show leaderboard]*
> "You can add your friends and compete on a leaderboard."

*[show streak]*
> "There's even a streak to keep you applying consistently."

**[CLOSE — back to a simple screen or your face briefly]**
> "The job hunt is already brutal. NoJob makes it feel like
> something you're actually progressing through."

> "Free. Link in bio."

---

**Editing notes:**
- Cut tight — every line should have a visual to match
- Use satisfying UI sounds when tapping through the app
- Text overlay at start: "I applied to 100+ jobs with zero system"
- End card: "@nojobapp — free download"
